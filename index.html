George Washington

The first president of the United States

Here's a time line of George Washington's life:

  • 1732 - Born in Bridges Creek, Colony of Virginia, British America
  • 1743 - Father dies when George is 11 years old
  • 1753 - Commissioned as Major to serve in the French and Indian war
  • 1755 - Became the senior American aide on the Braddock Expedition
  • 1759 - Married Martha Dandridge Custis
  • 1774 - Selected as Delegate to First Continental Congress
  • 1775 - Appointed as General and Commander-in-chief of Continental Army
  • 1776 - Defeated at the Battle of Long Island
  • 1776 - Crossing of the Delaware
  • 1780 - Victory at Yorktown
  • 1787 - Attends Constitutional Convention in Philadelphia
  • 1789 - Elected First President of the United States
  • 1792 - Reelected for President
  • 1796 - Washington's Farewell Address
  • 1799 - Death at the age of 67 in Mount Vernon, Virginia, U.S
